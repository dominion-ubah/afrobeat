The Falz Experience


Nigerian rap artist, Folarin Falana a.k.a Falz, has held his first headline concert in Lagos. Tagged ‘The Falz Experience’, the event took place on the Wednesday, December 20, at the Eko Convention Center, Eko Hotels & Suites. Top Nigerian acts like Simi, Yemi Alade, Davido, Reekado Banks, Wande Coal, Ycee, were among those who performed at the show.
